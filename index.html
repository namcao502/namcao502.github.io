Kamen Rider Decade (仮面ライダーディケイドＤＣＤ, Kamen Raidā Dikeido, "Masked Rider DCD"/"Masked Rider Decade") is a Japanese tokusatsu drama and the 19th entry of the Kamen Rider Series and the 10th entry of the Heisei Kamen Rider Series, commemorating the Heisei Kamen Rider 10th Anniversary and all of the Kamen Rider Series to date, but mostly the ones from Kamen Rider Kuuga onwards.
